# Logo v1 — Notes

## What's working

- **Color palette is strong.** The emerald green + gold accent combination feels premium and distinctive without being garish. Good contrast on both light and dark backgrounds.
- **Style is right.** Clean vector/flat aesthetic — modern enough for a SaaS app, not overly cartoonish or fantasy-heavy.
- **`genie_with_lamp.png` is the strongest composition.** The flowing smoke connecting the lamp to the genie figure gives it a natural visual hierarchy and reads well as a cohesive mark.
- **Transparent backgrounds** on the genie images — ready to drop onto any surface.

## What needs work

- **No Socratic element.** None of the images incorporate the question mark / inquiry concept. The genie looks like a wish-granting figure rather than a coach who asks good questions.
- **`lamp_only.jpg`** has the best lamp shape but the smoke is generic swirls — this is the best candidate to iterate on with the question-mark-in-smoke concept.
- **`genie_only.png`** is interesting but without the lamp it loses the "genie" context — just looks like an abstract swirl figure.
- **Favicon readability.** All three are too detailed for 32px. Need a simplified version for small sizes.

## Next steps

1. **Iterate on the lamp + question mark concept.** Use `lamp_only.jpg` as style reference but regenerate with smoke that clearly forms a question mark shape. See `docs/logo-prompt.txt` for the prompt.
2. **Try a version without the genie figure.** The lamp + question mark smoke may be strong enough on its own and will scale better to small sizes.
3. **Test at favicon size.** Whatever we land on needs to be legible at 32x32 and 16x16.
4. **Consider a simplified icon variant** — just the lamp silhouette for tight spaces, full composition for larger uses.
